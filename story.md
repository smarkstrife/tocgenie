# The Last Compiler

## Prologue

In the year 2077, the last human programmer sat alone in a dimly lit room.

## Chapter 1: The Bug

She stared at the screen. Line 4,521. The error had been there for three days.

### The Discovery

It was a missing semicolon.

### The Aftermath

She laughed for twenty minutes straight.

## Chapter 2: The Fix

One keystroke. The build passed. The world was saved.

## Epilogue

She pushed to main and went to sleep.
